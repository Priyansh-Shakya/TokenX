


# Weights

### Weights are Importance of each input value.
Wieght :- “How much should I care about this input?”

### For example:

**Question:**
Guess the number i am thinking , which is in range of 0 - 100.
**Hint:** Number is closer to 60.

### Now:
**Inputs:**
0 - 10 : Way Less Importance
11 - 20 : A little importance, but still Low
21 - 35 : High Importance
36 - 60 : Very High Importance
61 - 70 : High Importance
71 - 100 : Low Importance
#### Here , every input value will be multiplied with their weight (Importance) depending on which range they fall into.
